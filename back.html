Lat Pull Down
Targeted muscle: Lats, bicep

Instruction:

Sit on the seat and adjust the top pad so that it firmly holds your leg in place. Grab the bar at slightly wider than shoulder length or where you feel most comfortable and sit back down the seat in a deadhang position. Slowly pull the bar down to your upper chest and slightly lean back to activate your lats. On the way up, control the weight and ensure you go up as high as you can before performing your next rep.

Seated Cable Rows
Targeted muscle: lats, traps, rhomboids

Instruction:

Sit on the seat and place you feet on the pads. Have a slight bend in your knees and keep a neutral spine position. Have your shoulders down, chest out, and maintain a slight arc in your back. Slowly pull you arms to your torso, squeeze everything nice and tight, and slowly return the weight before starting your next rep.

Cable Lat Pullover
Targeted muscle: Lats

Instruction:

Choose an attachment for this workout and have it hooked onto the topmost section. Grab the attachment, take a few step back while keeping your arms fully extended. Place your feet shoulder width apart, bend your upper body foward, hinge hup back, slightly bend your knees, and push your shoulder blacdes forward. Pull the bar down and back towards torso, squeeze, and slowly release the back up.
