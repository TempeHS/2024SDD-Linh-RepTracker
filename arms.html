Arms
Cable Lateral Raise
Targeted muscle: Shoulder

Instruction:

Set up the cable machine with the handle being at around your quads and a D-handle attached to the cable. Stand sideways, grab the handle with one hand, slightly lean forward, and raise your arm to the side until it's parallel to the ground. Slowly lower it back down in a controlled manner. Keep your core stable and avoid any swaying movement to eliminate the use of momentum

Dumbbell Shoulder Press
Targeted muscle: Shoulder

Instruction:

Sit on the seat and place you feet on the pads. Have a slight bend in your knees and keep a neutral spine position. Have your shoulders down, chest out, and maintain a slight arc in your back. Slowly pull you arms to your torso, squeeze everything nice and tight, and slowly return the weight before starting your next rep.

Seated Dumbbell Curls
Targeted muscle: Bicep

Instruction:

Adjust the seta so that it is slightly reclined back. Sit down with both dumbbells in your hands and keep your arms in a neutral position. Start curling the dumbbell either simultaineously or alternatively and slowly release them back down to strech the muscle.
